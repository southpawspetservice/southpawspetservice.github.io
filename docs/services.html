Dog Walking Service

South Paws Pet Services is pleased to offer our customers personalized and compassionate pet care.

As a dog owner for 26 years I understand the need of dogs and other pets for love and affections on a daily basis. I also understand the sense of abandonment or separation anxiety that a pet can experience when left at home alone during the work day or at veterinarian kennels or boarding facilities.

These facilities can also become fairly expensive and can take away from vacation plans due to the higher costs and limited availabilities to accommodate your pet(s). It is well known that pets that get regular exercise tend to live longer and have healthier lives. It is our goal to provide you a better option for your pet(s) health and well being, your budget, and your sound piece of mind knowing that your pet(s) are being given quality personalized care.

Services

We provide the following services as well as basic pet care for vacation needs, daytime dog walks, and/or overnight pet and home care. We will do everything in our power to keep our clients coming back . Our goal is to love your pets as much as you do. When you trust your home and pets to our care, we take the responsibility very seriously.

Dog Walking

Includes 30 minutes of walking in your immediate neighborhood unless specified otherwise. We will walk in rain or snow.
Pet cleanup on walks is included in the base price.
For an additional fee (see rates), dogs may be transported to local dog parks or other outdoor destinations that are favorable for dog walking and play. Price includes transportation.

NEW!!! Doggie Day Care

Now offering doggie day care for clients who do not require overnight boarding. Clients can drop off for half day (less than 6 hours) or full day care.

Overnight In-home Pet Sitting

The base price for overnight stays in your home include: A late night potty break, late evening play, drinking water changeouts, a morning potty break, a morning feeding, and other in-home services regarding security. Overnight stays cost $10/night in addition to the base visit rates applied to that day of service. Overnight in-home pet care is scheduled based on staff availability, so please give us plenty of notice.

Daytime In-home Pet Sitting

Pet Play and Cuddle Time
Dogs love attention, especially when owners are away for a time. We can socialize with your animals and provide them the love and affection you would otherwise provide while you are away from you beloved pet(s). We will spend up to 30 minutes of play time with your pet(s) per visit.
Pet Feeding and Watering
During visits to your home, we can feed your pet(s) and give them fresh water to drink. Food and water supply is to be provided by the customer.
Mail and Newspaper Retrieval
As part of our home visit, we can retrieve any daily deliveries to your house for you and leave them in your home to give your home a "lived in" look during your absence.
House Lights, Curtains, and Plants
As part of our home visit, we can manage your lights and window blind/curtains during your vacations or business trips. As part of our home visit, we can water your plants. We also have a master gardener on staff for your indoor plant needs.

Yard Pet Waste Removal

We can remove pet waste from your yard for disposal on a regular basis anytime. Disposal bags are provided by South Paws Pet Service at no additional fee. We can also change out or clean up litterboxes during home visits. Litter is to be provided by the customer.
